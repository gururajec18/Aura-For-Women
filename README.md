🌸 AI-Based Menstrual Cycle, Pregnancy & PCOS Tracking System 🤖💖

In today’s fast-paced world, women’s health often goes unnoticed or untracked due to lack of awareness, time, and personalized guidance. To address this, we propose an AI-powered health tracking system focused on menstrual cycles, pregnancy, and PCOS management.

💡 Project Idea

This project aims to develop a smart digital platform that uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to monitor, predict, and guide women through different stages of their reproductive health.
